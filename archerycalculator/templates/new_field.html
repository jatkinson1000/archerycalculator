This page is a preview of the "new" Archery GB field classifications for iteration of score boundaries and rules prior to final release.

Questions:

  • Have the rounds been appropriately assigned for the updated bowstyes and age groups?
    • Should Archer tier be made available on shorter peg, "novice", rounds?

  • Should there be separate scores for Marked, Unmarked, and Mixed, or just one?
    • My feeling is that a single score for all rounds simplifies things.

  • Should there be an U21 category?
    • WA operates U21 and it exists for target classifications.

  • Should it be restricted to 24 target rounds?
    • Archer tier could be made available on 12-target passes. This would help with engagement, which is a key focus of the archer tier.

  • Should there be a restriction on number of courses?
    • Probably at MB level. How many places?
    • Should it be 'events' rather than courses, as many WA re-set between events?
    • From personal exp[erience it is restrictive if you live in certain locations.
    • Remove the ABO rule as has been same course twice in recent years?

  • Should AGB administer the highest level of awards?
